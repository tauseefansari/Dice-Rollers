🧾 Game Rules

  • 👉 You can start new game by clicking on "🔄 New game" button
  • 👉 You can roll a dice by clicking on "🎲 Roll dice" button
  • 👉 You can hold the score by clicking on "📥 Hold" button
  • 👉 Start the 🔄 New game with 0 Score and go upto 100 or more to win 🏆
  • 👉 Roll the Dice and Hold 📥 if you want otherwise when dice 🎲 is 1 your turn will over
  • 👉 Once you hold the Dice 🎲 current score will get added to Final score
  • 👉 Otherwise when your turn over your current score will be 0 again
  • 👉 Everytime when dice 🎲 is 1, Game switch to the next player
  • 👉 Who has reached to 100 and above score first, wins the Game 🏆

    • 🎉 Enjoy Playing the Game 🎊
